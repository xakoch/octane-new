Privacy Policy

The administration of the site octanefuel.com is committed to maintaining your privacy on the Internet. We attach great importance to protecting the data you provide. Our privacy policy is based on the requirements of the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). The purposes for which we collect personal data:

  • Improving the operation of our service;
  • Contacting visitors to this site;
  • Providing services related to the activities of this site;
  • As well as for the actions listed below.

Collection and Use of Personal Data

We collect and use your personal data only with your voluntary consent. By agreeing to this, you authorize us to collect and use the following data:

  • First and last name;
  • Email address;
  • Phone number.

SMS opt-in and phone numbers for the purpose of SMS will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. The collection and processing of your data is carried out in accordance with the laws in force in the European Union and the USA.

Data Storage, Modification and Deletion

The user who provided personal data to the site octanefuel.com has the right to change or delete it, as well as to withdraw consent to its use. Your data will be stored only for as long as necessary to fulfill the main activities of the site. Once you have finished using our services, the site administration will delete your data.

To access, modify or delete your personal data, please contact us at: info@octanefuel.com.

Use of Technical Data When Visiting the Site

When you visit octanefuel.com, we automatically log certain technical information in our database, including:

  • Your IP address;
  • Time of visit;
  • Browser settings;
  • Operating system;
  • Other technical details necessary for proper site display.

This data cannot be used to identify individual visitors.

Providing Information by Children

If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided personal information without your consent, please notify us at info@octanefuel.com. It is prohibited to leave minors’ personal data on our service without parental or guardian approval.

Use of Cookies

To ensure correct content display and improve usability on octanefuel.com, we use cookies—small files stored on your device. They help the site remember:

  • Your language preference;
  • Which pages you have visited;
  • Other browsing choices.

You can configure your browser to accept or block cookies. Disabling cookies may limit certain site functionalities.

Use of Personal Data by Other Services

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal data to any third party. You may review the privacy/user agreements of external services (e.g., Google Analytics, Yandex.Metrica) on their respective websites. The only exception is when disclosure is required by law or a government authority.

Links to Other Sites

Our website octanefuel.com may contain links to external sites not operated by us. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. We recommend reviewing each site’s privacy policy before providing any personal data.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in legislation or our practices. Any updates will be posted here. If you have provided personal data to us, we will notify you of important changes. Please ensure your contact information is accurate so we can reach you if needed.

Feedback and Final Provisions

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, you may contact us at info@octanefuel.com or via the contact form on our site. If you do not agree with this privacy policy, please refrain from using our services or visiting octanefuel.com.
